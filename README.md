# Yi Xiu Zhuan 🏯

A cultivation-themed auto-chess strategy game.

## Play Online

👉 https://spyyufish.github.io/yixiuzhuan-en/

## About

Recruit cultivators, activate faction synergies, merge to 3-star, and defeat bosses. Choose an artifact every major stage to empower your team!

- 10 factions · 40+ cultivators
- 6 formation types with unique bonuses
- Roguelike artifact system
- 12 major stages with increasing difficulty

## Development

Pure HTML5 / CSS / JavaScript, no dependencies needed.
